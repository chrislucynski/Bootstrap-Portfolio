About Me

Here I am with my wife and Cooper.

I live in Holladay, UT with my wife, Rachel, and our "fur baby," Cooper. My wife and I started dating in high school when our parents were stationed in Germany - my dad was in the Army, and her mom worked as a civilian for the Air Force. We then attended the University of Hawaii at Manoa together where we discovered our love for the islands.

Unfortunately, paradise comes with a price, so our careers brought us out to Utah where we've lived for the past 5 1/2 years, which is tied for the second-longest I've ever lived in one place consecutively. The beautiful landscape and scenery in Utah just took our breath away so we knew we had to set up roots here.

I'm currently working as a Nurse Manager for 5 West, the Inpatient Medical-Psychiatry unit at the University of Utah. We care for those individuals suffering from psychiatric issues (schizophrenia, psychosis, substance abuse/dependency/overdose, mood disorders) whom also have medical issues (wounds, diabetes, seizures, and organ disfunction). It is a very challenging job, but it can also be vastly rewarding to help others in their time of need. I have some veeeery interesting stories, so if you're interested in hearing them, come find and ask me!
